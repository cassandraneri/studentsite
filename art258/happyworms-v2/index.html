Easy Worm Bin Setup

STEP 1. PICK LOCATION

Select a shady area for your worm bin such as in your kitchen or a shady spot on your condo lanai. Ensure your bin has holes for air circulation.

STEP 2. ADD BEDDING

Add 4-6 inches of moist bedding made from coconut coir and shredded brown cardboard or shredded paper products (not bleached/colored).

STEP 3. ADD WORMS

Add worms to the bin including the bedding they were packed in. The worms will slowly work their way down to the bottom where it's nice and dark.

STEP 4. ADD FOOD WASTE

Feed chopped-up fruit and vegetable waste, coffee grounds, and grains in moderation such as rice, bread, cereal, and pasta.

*Save food scraps in a container in the fridge/freezer and feed once a week.

STEP 5. ADD CARBON

Cover the food waste completely with a layer of carbon rich materials such as shredded paper, newspaper, cardboard, dried leaves, or wood chips.

STEP 6. COVER BIN

Top the bin with a bin blanket of shadecloth. Cover with the plastic bin lid to keep it even more dark and cozy (optional).
